Dr. Divya Sekhar Vaka

Satellite Remote Sensing Analyst at GEOFEM

I am a doctoral graduate from IIT Bombay, specializing in Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) data analysis, with an emphasis on leveraging Earth Observation (EO) data for surface deformation studies. My academic journey has equipped me with advanced expertise in remote sensing techniques and their applications in environmental monitoring and hazard analysis.

Before embarking on my doctoral studies, I garnered valuable industry experience as a Software Analyst at Hexagon Geospatial, where I honed my analytical skills and gained practical insights into geospatial technologies.

Passionate about harnessing technology and data-driven approaches, I am enthusiastic about exploring opportunities that allow me to apply my expertise, drive impactful projects, and contribute to the advancement of remote sensing applications in disaster management, infrastructure assessment, and environmental sustainability.
